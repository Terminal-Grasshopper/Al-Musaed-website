Al Musaed: Talk to the Future

Bringing the power of AI to Fujairah's municipal services.

What is Al Musaed?

Al Musaed is a cutting-edge 100% local AI-powered solution developed by the UOWD Student Team, designed to enhance engagement and streamline services across any department. It works seamlessly as a text-based chatbot, voice assistant, or a 3D avatar, providing flexible interaction modes for diverse use cases.

What are the Features of Al Musaed?

Data Security & Privacy

Unlike most AI tools that rely on cloud servers and API, Al Musaed runs 100% locally within Fujairah Municipality's secure systems. This ensures that all data stays under local control, providing enhanced privacy and security

Interactive 3D Avatar

Al Musaed’s next-generation 3D avatar brings a human touch to digital interaction, responding in real-time with realistic gestures, facial expressions, and voice communication. Whether it's in a public space, corporate setting, or customer service environment, the avatar creates an engaging, immersive experience for users.

Multilingual Capabilities

Designed to bridge language gaps, Al Musaed is equipped with multilingual support, including Arabic, English, Hindi, German and many more. This ensures smooth communication with diverse audiences, making it accessible for people from different linguistic backgrounds.

Accurate Information Delivery

With Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology at its core, Al Musaed guarantees precise and reliable information. While typical chatbots rely on data searches, Al Musaed uses semantic search, to understand the meaning behind user queries. This ensures accurate and reliable responses.

Customizable Persona

Al Musaed offers a highly flexible 3D avatar that can be tailored to meet the specific needs of various industries. Customize the avatar’s appearance, voice, and language to align with your organization’s identity, whether it’s for customer service, education, or tourism purposes.

Powered by Students

We are a passionate team of students from the University of Wollongong in Dubai, proud to have developed this innovative AI-powered solution in collaboration with Fujairah Municipality. Throughout the summer, we worked tirelessly to bring our vision to life, overcoming challenges with limited resources but boundless determination. This project is a testament to what can be achieved through collaboration, creativity, and a passion for pushing the boundaries of technology.
