Overview of AI in IT Support

Artificial intelligence has transformed various industries by automating tasks, boosting efficiency, and enhancing user experiences. In IT support, AI plays a critical role by providing functionalities such as automated ticketing, predictive maintenance, and intelligent virtual assistants, reducing downtime and improving service management.

Importance of Local Solutions for Municipalities

Local AI solutions cater to specific needs, including regional workflows, regulatory requirements, and language preferences. Implementing AI locally ensures better user adoption and enhanced data security, which is crucial for handling confidential government information.
